EXHIBIT B: Very Big Things, LLC. STATEMENT OF WORK

**INTRODUCTION/BACKGROUND**

The following Statement of Work (SOW) shall serve to outline the current
scope of work required for \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_ (“Client”), involving Very
Big Things, LLC (“VBT”) and is made pursuant to the Master Service
Agreement between the parties dated \_\_\_\_\_\_, 2025 (the “MSA”).

**SCOPE**

Consumer Profiling & Data Intelligence project focusing on transforming
Client's disconnected customer data into actionable intelligence through
the creation of 4-5 data-driven consumer personas. The project will
analyze customer information from Shopify, Meta Ads, KnoCommerce,
Klaviyo, and Amazon to reveal distinct customer segments based on
purchasing behavior, demographics, and preferences, providing Client
with a clear framework for targeting marketing campaigns, crafting
relevant messaging, and optimizing paid media spend.

**Processes & Deliverables:**

- <u>Data Foundation & Integration</u>: Receive, organize, clean, and
  > standardize data across all client platforms (Shopify, Meta Ads,
  > KnoCommerce, Klaviyo, Amazon) to create a unified customer dataset
  > for comprehensive analysis.

- <u>Customer Behavior Analysis</u>: Conduct exploratory data analysis
  > including demographics assessment, purchase pattern identification,
  > and customer journey mapping to identify meaningful segmentation
  > opportunities.

- <u>Persona Development</u>: Execute clustering and segmentation
  > algorithms to identify distinct customer segments and develop 4-5
  > detailed consumer personas with comprehensive demographics,
  > behaviors, and preferences profiles.

- <u>Python Notebooks</u>: Provide complete data cleaning and
  > preprocessing scripts, exploratory data analysis code,
  > clustering/segmentation algorithms, and statistical analysis
  > supporting persona identification.

- <u>Presentation & Documentation</u>: Deliver comprehensive
  > presentation including detailed consumer personas with data-backed
  > rationale, actionable insights for marketing application, and
  > methodology documentation for future replication.

- <u>Supplementary Resources</u>: Provide AI prompts and methodology
  > guide for future analysis, key findings summary, and recommendations
  > for data collection improvements to enhance ongoing customer
  > intelligence efforts.

- <u>Knowledge Transfer</u>: Conduct knowledge transfer session(s) to
  > ensure Client team understands analysis methodology, persona
  > applications, and operational procedures for maintaining customer
  > intelligence insights.

**OUT OF SCOPE**

The following items are explicitly excluded from the scope of this SOW:
building automated data pipelines, creating real-time dashboards,
implementing a Customer Data Platform (CDP), ongoing persona maintenance
or updates, integration with existing systems, and custom software
development. This engagement focuses solely on the analysis and delivery
of static consumer personas and supporting documentation.

**ESTIMATED COST & TIMELINE**

The cost to complete this Consumer Profiling project is \$17,000, with
an estimated timeline of 4 weeks for completion from the agreed upon
start date.

**DEPOSIT**

VBT is currently in possession of \$5,000, which is being held as a
deposit under the prior SOW. That \$5,000 shall be applied and held as a
deposit under this SOW. If at any time the Client wishes to terminate
this SOW pursuant to the MSA, the deposit shall be applied to all
outstanding hours committed by VBT, and the remaining deposit amount, if
any, shall be refunded to the Client.  Upon completion of the work to be
performed under this SOW the deposit, at the Client’s discretion, may
either be returned to the Client or applied to any upcoming invoice or
future work.

**INVOICING**

Payment shall be invoiced net 15 days, with invoices sent at the
beginning of each two-week billing cycle for the pro rata cost of such
billing cycle.

**SCOPE**

Phase 2 Development - Complete modernization and rebuild of the entire
ClearPack application on a contemporary, scalable architecture while
maintaining full feature parity with the current platform. The project
will rebuild the platform on a modern tech stack (.NET + Angular),
optimize performance by removing current bottlenecks, preserve all core
modules and business logic, and establish scalability foundations for
long-term extensibility and reporting capabilities.

The following core modules will be re-architected and fully
reimplemented as part of this SOW:

1.  **Business Relationship Management Module** - Design and implement
    core business entity management system.

- Full CRUD for Clients, Suppliers, and Facilities

- Contact-level classification and role assignment

- Scoped permissions based on contact type and role

- User account management for internal/external users

- Facility-to-supplier linkage

- Admin-managed role definitions (not editable via UI)

2.  **Product Specification Module** - Develop comprehensive quality
    testing specification management system.

- Product line CRUD, category/subcategory assignment

- Exportable facility mappings

- Parameter management with impact categories

- Versioned templates and specifications

- Revert-to-previous version capabilities

- Mapping of specs to product lines

- Bulk tools for finding orphans and auditing usage

- Admin panel for global test properties (units, dimensions, etc.)

3.  **NOTR Management System** - Build Notification of Testing
    Requirements functionality for recurring quality testing events.

- Calendar-based NOTR creation (automated or ad hoc)

- Retry logic on failed generations

- Assignment of contacts to NOTRs

- Linking of NOTRs to product lines, projects, and facilities

- Entry of lab results (including image uploads)

- Full audit trail of each interaction with the NOTR

- CoA (Certificate of Analysis) generation from completed NOTRs

- Manual CoA verification and publishing workflows

- Scheduled CoA distribution on the 25th (or designated time) of each
  > month

4.  **Quality Issue Management Module** - Implement centralized quality
    issue management consolidating Corrective Actions and Significant
    Non-Conformances.

- Rule-based auto-ticket creation (e.g., failed tests trigger issues)

- Manual creation of tickets from within ClearPack

- Rule engine for defining thresholds and issue severity

- One-way syncing of ticket status from external system

- Linking of issues to NOTRs, test results, and specifications

- Power BI dashboard to view open issues by client/supplier

- Monthly reporting of unresolved quality issues

5.  **Invoicing Module** - Create comprehensive invoice generation and
    management system.

- Automatic invoice generation from completed or missed NOTRs

- Manual and bulk invoice creation

- Editable line items and invoice-level adjustments

- Invoice publishing and locking

- Track the *create* *date* and *sent* *date* of invoices

- Payment logging (manual entry, partial payments, check numbers, audit
  > trail)

- Support for payments spanning multiple invoices

- Excel exports for single invoices and invoice lists

- Supplier-specific branding (color, logo, layout)

- Pricing Data editor with client/category mapping

- Retry logic and historical logs for generation failures

- Create PO Requests

**ESTIMATED COST & TIMELINE**

The standard hourly rate charged by VBT has increased to \$300/hr. (the
“Standard Rate”). In order to maintain the prior hourly rate of
\$245/hr. (the “Prior Rate”), Client has agreed to engage VBT to perform
the work detailed in this SOW, and VBT has committed to billing work
under this SOW at the Prior Rate. Additionally, to reflect the parties’
on-going partnership, VBT has agreed to provide a 20% discount on the
work done under this SOW. Therefore, work performed under this SOW shall
be billed hourly at a rate of \$196/hr. and invoiced in monthly billing
cycles. The Prior Rate and discount shall only apply to the work
performed under this SOW.

The estimated costs and timeline to complete Phase 2 Development are
detailed below:

- Estimated Hours: 3,250 - 3,500 hours

- Estimated Cost: \$637,000 - \$686,000 (reflecting VBT's committed 20%
  > discount)

- Estimated Timeline: 9 - 10 months

The cost of the ClearPack Phase 2 Development shall not exceed \$686,000
without prior authorization from the Client.

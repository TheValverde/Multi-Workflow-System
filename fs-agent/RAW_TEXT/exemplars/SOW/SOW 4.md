EXHIBIT A: Very Big Things, LLC. STATEMENT OF WORK

**INTRODUCTION/BACKGROUND**

The following Statement of Work (SOW) shall serve to outline the current
scope of work required for \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_ (“Client”), involving Very
Big Things, LLC (“VBT”) and is made pursuant to the Master Service
Agreement between the parties dated October \_\_\_\_, 2025 (the “MSA”).

**SCOPE**

AI Sprint: This AI Sprint phase shall analyze The Shade Store’s current
business challenges to uncover areas of high potential ROI through the
implementation of generative AI.

**Processes:** VBT shall provide the following Services, each in
accordance with the terms and conditions of the MSA and based on
Client’s specifications:

- <u>Stakeholder Workshops</u>: Engage with key stakeholders of Client
  > to identify current workflows with a high potential ROI for the
  > implementation of generative AI.

- <u>AS-IS State Mapping</u>: Analysis of current operational processes
  > to gain insights into current challenges.

- <u>Path Forward</u>: Creation of a detailed report identifying the top
  > two to three areas for the implementation of generative AI and
  > providing a clear path forward, including outlining certain “off the
  > shelf” solutions and custom builds (as applicable), the potential
  > benefits of such solutions, and the particular processes such
  > solutions should streamline.

**COST & TIMELINE**

The cost to complete this AI Sprint phase is \$40,000, with an estimated
timeline of 6 weeks for completion.

**DEPOSIT**

A deposit equal to \$15,000, shall be paid within ten (10) business days
of the execution of this SOW. If at any time the Client wishes to
terminate this SOW pursuant to the MSA, the deposit shall be applied to
all outstanding hours committed by VBT, and the remaining deposit
amount, if any, shall be refunded to the Client within ten (10) business
days of Client’s notice to VBT of its intention to terminate the SOW.
Further, the deposit shall, if Client elects, be applied to the last
payment due under this SOW.  Upon completion of the work to be performed
under this SOW the deposit, at the Client’s discretion, may either be
returned to the Client or applied to any upcoming invoice (including the
last invoice for this SOW) or future work**.**

**INVOICING**

Payment shall be invoiced net 30 days with invoices sent at the
beginning of each two-week billing cycle for the pro rata cost of such
billing cycle. Invoices shall be paid in accordance with the terms of
the MSA.

For the avoidance of doubt, the fee due for the Services under this SOW
shall be \$40,000 regardless of if the number of hours to complete the
Services multiplied by the hourly rate is less than or greater than
\$40,000.

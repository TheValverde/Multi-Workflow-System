**INTRODUCTION/BACKGROUND**

The following Statement of Work (SOW) shall serve to outline the current
scope of work required for \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_ (“Client”),

**SCOPE**

AI Sprint: This AI Sprint phase is aimed at analyzing your current
business challenges to uncover areas of high potential ROI through the
implementation of generative AI.

**Processes**

- <u>Stakeholder Workshops</u>: Engage with key stakeholders to identify
  > current workflows with a high potential ROI for the implementation
  > of generative AI.

- <u>AS-IS State Mapping</u>: Analysis of current operational processes
  > to gain insights into current challenges.

- <u>Path Forward</u>: Creation of a detailed report identifying the top
  > three to four areas for the implementation of generative AI and
  > providing a clear path forward.
